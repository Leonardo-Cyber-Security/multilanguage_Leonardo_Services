    Business perimeters certification

    Global Cyber ​​​​Competence Center Perimeter

    Cybersecurity management for: design, integration, maintenance, installation, and support of managed cybersecurity systems, including SOC (Security Operation Center) services, Red Team, Cyber ​​Threat Intelligence, CSIRT (Computer Security Incident Response Team), PSIRT (Product Security Incident Response Team), information systems development, management, and maintenance services, digital identity management, application and perimeter security, and professional support services; design, integration, and delivery of IT and security solutions;

    Design and delivery of consulting services in the areas of Security Governance and Privacy, Cyber ​​Resilience, Hosting and Application Management of ERP systems (SAP), Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity, Crisis Recovery Vault, Technical Service Desk, NOC (Management of LAN/WAN network equipment and related security systems), Zero Trust network management, 24-hour monitoring, and System Management (management of infrastructure servers). Provision of Secure Cloud Computing services, using the ISO/IEC 27017:2015 and 27018:2019 guidelines for IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS cloud computing services. In accordance with the Declaration of Applicability, version dated October 14, 2025, and through the application of the ISO/IEC 27701:2019 and ISO/IEC 27035-1,2:2016 Guidelines. (Reference to Certificate No. 249744-2017-AIS-ITA-ACCREDIA).

    Cyber ​​& Security Solutions Engineering Perimeter

    Design, development, management, and maintenance of solutions, products, and services, as well as other activities and related transversal Engineering processes, such as governance, configuration management, and methodologies, to support business activities and the integration and validation of solutions and products.

    Operation of IaaS and PaaS cloud applications using the ISO/IEC 27017:2015 and 27018:2019 guidelines. In accordance with the Declaration of Applicability, version dated October 14, 2025. (Reference to Certificate No. 249744-2017-AIS-ITA-ACCREDIA).
